Figth Night

“Fight Night Combat returns to the EJC for the 16th year. Do you want to watch the best jugglers battle for 3 club combat supremacy? The finals are 11pm Thursday in the 24 hour hall. Do you have what it takes to compete?

Fight Night is a bracket-based competition of Combat Juggling; the last juggler maintaining their pattern wins! In order to compete you do need to participate in a qualification round. After you finish your qualification round you will need to stay around in order to play everyone and to see if you have qualified to play in the Knockout Tournament. Qualifications are open to ALL jugglers at 4pm Thursday in the main hall.”

Olympic Games

After the welcome parade, the juggling games led by the “Carampa” circus school are held, the Madrid school will offer us fun games to share between jugglers and the town of Tres Cantos. It will be a very fun moment.
Don’t miss it because we will do the spectacular and traditional juggling throw!
Sunday 7, 8:00 p.m. in the Family Square

Parade

Take part in the exceptional, colorful, dancey parade. Let’s show the inhabitants of Tres Cantos the amazing energy and the joy of the juggling community! Take a nice costume, make a balloon sculpture, juggle, ride a unicycle, play the drum, do whatever comes to your mind, so as to help us transfer the atmosphere of the EJC onto Tres Cantos streets. 

Sunday 7th from Tres Cantos Council Square at 18:00 h 

Street Shows

During the convention we will enjoy 4 street shows. The El Fedito company will present its Oyún show on Monday the 8th. Also on Wednesday the 10th, it will be the turn for “Copileft” in the Antonio Gala square.

The Argentine-born artist Chacobache will perform at the Arena on Thursday the 11th and on Friday the 12th, Endika Salazar will be present in the Antonio Gala square. Confirm the schedules at the info point.

Expos And Conferences

A space and different conversations could not be missing to learn about different social circus projects from Europe and the world. We will have the participation of different groups.

QUACK! CIRCUS THEATER COMMUNITY COLOMBIA: Collective that was very present in the great explosion of Colombia and that uses the circus as a sociocultural tool.
FEECSE: Federation of circus schools in Spain. They will explain their project and will have an area in the festival where they perform adapted circus.
CIRCORED. Digital and training platform. They will have their own stand together with a 24h info point and they will also present their project in the conference area.
MMCC Global. The social circus project born in 2002 in Afghanistan, seeks to help young people in war zones through social circus. We will be able to see the photographic exhibition in the 24h sports center and they will also present the project.

Workshops

Throughout the day in the central park, you can give or receive different workshops. In addition, the 24-hour space will also host workshops in its facilities. We will have different areas for you to enjoy to the fullest. This is a moment of encounter, where to share. Everyone is welcome. You can find all the information about the workshops on our big blackboard and in the info point.

Gandini studio will give a workshop on 8-9-10 at 11 in the morning in room X of the 24-hour sports center. To sign up you have to ask at the info point.

Physical Score Workshop (Maku Fanchulini) August 10, 4:00 p.m. in the diverse circus room in the 24h sports center.

Open Stage

The Open Stage is the place where you can show your talent. A space open to new proposals. If you want to participate in the Open Stage, come to the open assembly on Sunday 7 at 11:00, where you can be selected to participate. The freshest performances are guaranteed.

The Open Stage is a place where you’ll be able to showcase your talent. A space open to new ideas. If you want to participate in the Open Stage, come to the Open Assembly on Sudnay 7th @11am. You can get selected to participate. You’ll have the chance to see the freshest performances yet.

The Arena Stage

The Arena is a space full of magic. Located next to Tres Cantos lake. Every night it will host different shows. In the Arena, music and fire cannot be missed every day. Enjoy the grand gala and the fire open stage, in addition to all the musical programming with Dj Rambla, Sharin Tropikal, Rapha pico or Dj Bubba among others. The sky of Tres Cantos will shine with the most avant-garde fire shows. Do not miss the great fire gala on Thursday 12 from 11:00 p.m. Check the schedule and come and enjoy our delicious cocktails. This area is sounded by an artisan sound system, they come from the province of Segovia and are called AJO NEGRO, check the programming and go enjoy.

Big Gala

On Saturday the 13th we will enjoy the great gala. A very special show, where we will have the performances of high-level international artists. An opportunity to experience the world of the circus and juggling in all its splendor. Cyr wheel, bouncing balls, unicycle, hoops and much more by artists such as Katya Nikiforova, the Japanese Nano Kamata and Shoya Munakata, Zak Mcallister from Philadelphia, the company Cía Les Objets Volants, Helena Humm, Marica Marinoni or the Nicanor de Elia company. In addition, the great gala will be presented by the legendary and incomparable Wilbur.

Special Shows

Within the programming, we have also included some special shows. We want to surprise you with this program. To begin with, we will have the Mediterranean gala to open the convention on Sunday the 7th in the Foresta tent.

Also the show that we have prepared, With Skirts and crazy, as well as the performances of Circanario or Spinish Circo. Check the schedule at the info point to confirm the time and place of each performance.
